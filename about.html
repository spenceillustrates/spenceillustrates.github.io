About & Contact Portfolio
Spencer is an illustrator and designer based in Birmingham, United Kingdom with a passion for history, nature and all things deep and curious. With a background in English Literature, his practice mainly lies within narrative and storytelling. Atmosphere, detail and emotion are at the centre of his work, alongside a passion for interesting characters, animals and fantastical settings.
Spencer graduated with a First in Illustration at Birmingham City University, and won Doaly's Graphic Illustration award.
