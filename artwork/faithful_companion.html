Faithful Companion
A personal illustration inspired and fuelled by my adoration of horses.
The bond between humans and horses runs deep throughout history. Humanity has relied on horses and other equine creatures for transport, war and labour which has since transformed into sport, leisure and therapy in equestrian culture.
Horses, to me, are the most soulful animals on the planet. They're prey animals with a strong heart. This illustration encapsulates the spirit of the warrior and horse relationship in high-fantasy narratives. Despite the dangers the warrior overcomes with the skills of endurance and strategy, the trust of their horse cannot be dismissed.
