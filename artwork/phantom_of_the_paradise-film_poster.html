Phantom of the Paradise - Film Poster
A digital illustration of a concept for a poster for the 1974 horror musical, Phantom Of The Paradise.
I was very inspired by the campy 70s glam-rock theme of the film. I wanted this poster to feel as bizarre and maximalist as the narrative, with bright colours and strobe lights flowing in every direction!
Within illustrating film posters, I tend to adopt a semi-realistic style instead of completely conforming to hyper-realism. Illustrating real-life actors and film characters is always a fun challenge!
